WHY US

Your home away from home

Off street secure parking in front of each guest room, grounds are secure with high wall and electric fencing. Breakfast, Dinner & Lunch Packs offered at an additional fee, served in our dining hall (30 seats).

There is access to a swimming pool and lapa with a braai facilities and free WiFi. Become part of the African Sky Family knowing that your health, safety and comfort is our priority!
