Frequently Asked Questions

To book a stay, visit our website and use the "Book Online" button on the room details page. Alternatively, you can contact us directly at +27 73 230 1337 or via email at bibbys.gh@gmail.com to check availability and make a reservation. A deposit may be required to secure your booking.

Check-in is from 13:00 (01:00 PM), and check-out is until 10:00 AM. If you need an early check-in or late check-out, please contact us in advance, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your request based on availability.

Cancellations made at least 2 days before your arrival date are fully refundable. If you cancel within 2 days of your arrival, a cancellation fee may apply. Please refer to our cancellation policy on the booking page or contact us for more details.

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed at Bibby's B&B to ensure the comfort of all guests. We appreciate your understanding.

Each room includes a kitchenette, tea and coffee set, 40" TV, safe, bathroom with shower, towels, uncapped Wi-Fi, and wall heaters, electric blankets. Free parking is also available with a reservation.
