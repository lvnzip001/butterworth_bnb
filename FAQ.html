FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

To book a stay, visit our website and use the "Book Now" button on the room details page. Alternatively, you can contact us directly at +27 73 230 1337 or via email at info@bibbysbnb.co.za to check availability and make a reservation. A deposit may be required to secure your booking.

Check-in is from 14:00 (2:00 PM), and check-out is until 11:00 AM. If you need an early check-in or late check-out, please contact us in advance, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your request based on availability.

Cancellations made at least 7 days before your arrival date are fully refundable. For cancellations within 7 days, a 50% cancellation fee may apply. Please contact us directly to cancel or modify your booking.

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed at Bibby's BnB to ensure the comfort of all guests. We appreciate your understanding.

Each room includes a kitchenette, tea and coffee set, 32" UHD TV, safe, bathroom with shower, towels, air conditioning, hairdryer, uncapped Wi-Fi, and access to Netflix and YouTube. Free parking is also available with a reservation.
